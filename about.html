--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About I am part of the Bioinformatics team at Genomics England Previously I was the first Data hire at Arkera and built the foundation of their Data platform. Before that I was the first Data hire at Stylect where I built their first recommender system for their app and website, utilising my expertise in Machine Learning. In December 2015 I spoke at Big Data Week 2015, co-hosting the workshop: "How to Become an Effective Data Scientist". I transitioned into industry through a Postdoctoral Fellow on the Data Science & Business Analytics Fellowship offered by The ASI. I was previously a Postdoctoral Research Associate at MIT. I completed my Ph.D. at the University of Cambridge (you can spot me in the bottom left corner here in the 60th Anniversary photo of my former research group) where my thesis won the Springer Thesis Prize: "Recognising Outstanding Ph.D. Research" and is published in the Springer Thesis Program.
